Grace

Grace is a fast-paced survival horror game where players must sprint through endless rooms while trying to survive dangerous entities and a constantly ticking timer.

The game focuses on movement, speed, and quick decision-making, forcing players to rush from one saferoom to the next before time runs out. Each run is different because the rooms are procedurally generated, meaning you never know what’s coming next.

Inspired by games like DOORS and Pizza Tower, Grace combines horror with fast movement mechanics, making it feel more intense and chaotic than traditional Roblox horror games. The deeper you go, the more difficult and unpredictable the game becomes.
